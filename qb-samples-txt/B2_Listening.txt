@Part 1 Questions 1-8 (8 pts)
##
You will hear people talking in eight different situations. For questions 1 - 8, choose the best answer (A, B or C).
//
Question 1. You hear a woman talking about telling jokes. What does she say is her problem?
C. She cannot make people laugh.
A. She cannot remember jokes.
B. She does not hear or read many jokes.
Question 2. You hear a man talking about writing books. What is he proud of?
B. He has adapted to writing on a computer.
A. He started writing at a young age.
C. He has published two books.
Question 3. You hear two people talking about children’s books. Where are they?
B. at school
A. in a bookshop
C. at home
Question 4. You hear two people talking about a new drama series. How does the man feel about it?
B. impressed
A. disappointed
C. confused
Question 5. You hear a voicemail message about a new job. What is the speaker doing?
C. making a request
A. making a recommendation
B. making an offer
Question 6. You hear a woman talking about a lie she told. Why did she tell the lie?
B. She was bored with swimming.
A. Her mum did not like her friends.
C. She needed the money.
Question 7. You hear two people talking about a writing competition. Why is the man concerned?
B. He wrote his entry too quickly.
A. He wrote about the wrong topic.
C. He sent his entry in too late.
Question 8. You hear a voicemail message. What is the woman complaining about?
A. a replaced actor
B. missing tickets

@Part 4  Questions 24 - 30 (7 pts)
##
You will hear a man talking on a radio programme about changes he has noticed in his hometown.
For questions 24 - 30, choose the best answer (A, B or C).
//
Question 24. Tommy has not returned to his home town for a long time because
C. he no longer has close relatives in the area.
A. he has had to devote all his time to acting.
B. there are some aspects of his life there he is ashamed of.

Question 25. What contributed most to the changes in Tommy’s attitude to life?
A. becoming a celebrity
B. being a long way from home
C. not having his friends around him
Question 26. How has the centre of Tommy’s home town changed?
C. The road system has been altered.
A. The shops there are much bigger.
B. It offers more facilities for families.

Question 27. How does Tommy feel about the changes made to the road where he used to live?
A. He regrets the loss of green spaces.
B. He is concerned about overcrowding in the area.
C. He is full of admiration for the design of the new buildings.
Question 28. What did Tommy use to do in his free time?
C. He did open-air sporting activities.
A. He walked other people’s dogs for them.
B. He met up with his friends outside the shops.

Question 29. Why did Tommy’s parents want him to join the drama group?
A. They thought it would keep him out of trouble.
B. They felt he had real acting talent.
C. They wanted to pass on their love of the theatre.
Question 30. What does Tommy say he has heard about his old school?
B. People think more highly of it now.
A. The sports facilities have improved.
C. The students are more involved in looking after the gardens.
